Product Name:
Eyes First – Maze

Description:
Eyes First - Maze is a path finding game with four maze sizes of increasing difficulty. You may choose to play a randomized maze with 3, 4, 5 or 6 rows. The goal of the game is to navigate the dog back to the dog house in as few moves as possible. 
This app is also designed to be played with eye control using a Windows 10 compatible eye tracker. 


Product features:
